Our Story

KrishiPredict was born from a simple observation: while technology was advancing rapidly in urban sectors, our farmers - the backbone of our nation - were still relying on traditional methods and guesswork.

Founded in 2024, we set out to bridge this gap by developing an intelligent system that could analyze multiple environmental factors and provide accurate predictions for crop yield and risk assessment.

Our name "KrishiPredict" combines "Krishi" (Agriculture in Sanskrit) with "Predict," representing our commitment to bringing predictive analytics to Indian farming.

Today, we're proud to serve farmers across Maharashtra, helping them make data-driven decisions for better agricultural outcomes.
